Who We Are

We build people the same way we build houses.

Hayward Contracting is a family-run trade business, not a staffing company. We hire carpenters who want a home — people who take pride in craft, show up every day, and grow with the company.

Accountability Without Politics

We say what we mean, fix what we break, and give credit where it's due. No layers of management between the crew and the owner.

Craft as a Standard, Not a Selling Point

The Hayward Method isn't a tagline — it's how we train every carpenter from day one. Consistency builds reputations.

Long-Term Employment

W2, not 1099. Stable schedules, consistent pay, and a team that looks out for each other. We're not a gig — we're a career.

Recognition That Means Something

Top crews get recognized. We celebrate milestones, reward performance, and invest in people who invest in the work.
