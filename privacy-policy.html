Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 3, 2026

Hayward Contracting ("we," "us," or "our"), a division of Hayward, Inc., is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit haywardcontracting.com or contact us.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Name, email address, and phone number submitted through our contact form

We use the information you provide to:

If you provide your phone number and opt in to receive text messages from Hayward Contracting, the following terms apply:

By opting in, you agree to receive text messages related to conversational purposes from Hayward Contracting at the phone number provided. SMS frequency may vary. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out of receiving text messages at any time. For assistance, text HELP to (910) 937-7767.

We do not sell or share your phone number with third parties for marketing purposes. Your consent to receive SMS messages is not a condition of purchasing any goods or services.

Sharing of Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information with trusted service providers (such as our form processing provider, Formspree) solely to operate our website and respond to your inquiries.

Data Retention

We retain your contact information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purpose for which it was collected, or as required by law.

Your Rights
